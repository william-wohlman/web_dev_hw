About Me

I am a Fine Art's student in my senior year here at the University of Montana. I was interested in the way that some artists portray themselves and their creativity through their gallery sites. I work primarilly digitally so this was not a leap for me to invest in.

As a Fine art's student, I have been educated in Figure drawing. As an artist, also really love figure drawing and the drawing of animals in general. I have learned of and picked up several tools and tips as I have kept up my independant studies. The Art Tips section of this site is actually personal tennants and observations I have gained through experience.

Links to Check Out

Chris Oatley

Check out Chris Oatley on Youtube and at Oatley academy. I haven't taken many courses with them but they're affordable and informative. Not to mention the community is very supportive!

Bobby Chiu

Bobby Chiu spearheads Schoolism, an online art learning platform, where you enroll in classes offered by professional artists. You can learn quite a lot and gain feedback from people who've worked professionally for years. I havent had the time or money to invest but Chiu's work is amazing and his youtube videos are full of insight.

Physical Sessions

If you are looking for physical model sessions that are fairly cheap, local art meusams or guilds may be the way to go. The Missoula Art Museum offers these classes.
